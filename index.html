Manholes Cover

More and more technology is used to realise various connections to networks by setting cables under the ground. And manhole like a small door to enter the underground. We benefit from the network system under the ground and save both public and living space. Also, data runs between different areas through the cables of the underground.

Power Line

Every family is connected by powerline to support electricity supply and their daily life, from electric lights to electric furniture, power lines are essential to everyone. Meanwhile, not only power supply but also help connect to the network.

Cables

Cables are found everywhere throughout the whole wellington city and are particularly concentrated near the university and central CBD, for students and workers who have big demand on the Internet, they cannot work or learn without the internet. Also, these cables contribute to realising faster and more stable internet connection.

Antenna

These antennae are easily found in every neighborhood. Although we use cables and powerlines to connect to the internet, many families still rely on old antennae, for it makes the internet more acceptable and achievable. They providing various possibilities for networking.

CCTV

We are living in a digital and big data times, so CCTV is almost everywhere, especially regards to entries of some banks, restaurants, supermarkets, and shopping centers. CCTV is mainly used as a piece of security equipment to recording public space, and then sending data back to networks to ensure people's safety and provide some technical supports.
